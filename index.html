Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Luvium Floor Plan Financing.

What is floor plan financing?

It is a revolving line of credit that allows dealerships to purchase inventory (vehicles). The credit line is secured by the inventory itself, freeing up your operational cash flow.

How do I qualify for a credit line?

We look at your dealership'\''s sales history, inventory turnover, and market location. Our AI-driven onboarding process minimizes paperwork and speeds up approval.

Which regions do you cover?

While we operate across South Africa, our primary mission is to support dealerships in rural and underserved areas that are often neglected by major metropolitan banks.

Is there a minimum turnover requirement?

We assess each dealer individually. Our goal is to empower growth, so we consider your potential and local market dynamics alongside historical data.
